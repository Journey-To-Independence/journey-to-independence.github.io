1857-1885

The revolt of 1857 - India's First War of Independence or Sepoy Mutiny started on 10th May,1857 at Meerut marked the beginning of a protracted freedom struggle. Even though it was unsuccessful due to some reasons, it lasted for a year.

1915-1916

The Indigo Revolt - Started in March 1859, it was a peaceful strike by the ryots of a village in Bengal's Nadia region, who collectively vowed to refuse to plant any more indigo.

1919-1920

Establishment of Indian national congress - On 28th December 1885, the Indian National Congress was founded at a prestigious College in Bombay, with 72 delegates in attendance and the aim to be the nation's voice and presenting demands on national level.
